Here are the directions for the vinyl decals.

Make sure the project surface is clean and dry.
Peel the vinyl liner (white) away from the transfer tape and design.
If your vinyl doesn't separate from the liner, simply rub the liner back onto the vinyl and peel away again.
Attach a small area of design to your surface.
Use a scraper (something like a credit card) to rub the design onto your surface
TIP Designs with smaller details, like RJ, can take time; be patient.
Peel the transfer tape away from the design.
If the design peels up with transfer tape, simply rub the transfer tape and vinyl back down to the project surface and peel away again.

MOST IMPORTANTLY: Have fun and take care of each other!!
